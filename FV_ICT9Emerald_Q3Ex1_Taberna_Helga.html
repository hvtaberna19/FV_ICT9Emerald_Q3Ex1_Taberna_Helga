MY FAVORITE DISNEY CHARACTERS

  • Belle

    Belle is a Disney princess from the story Beauty and the Beast. Her beauty, elegance, and her trait of being adventerous is why she is one of my favorite Disney characters.


  • Baymax

    Baymax is a character from the film Big Hero 6. He is a inflatable robot that was built by one of the characters who sadly passed away. He stands as a personal health care assistant and helps others in all situations.


  • Spiderman

    Spiderman is a Marvel character from several films. A teenager was bitten by a spider and this is where the story of Spiderman began.
